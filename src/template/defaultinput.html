HTML5 Slide Maker

A live HTML5 slideshow editor using NodeJS

Getting started

It's easy. Just change the code on the left and the preview will be updated instantly!

Save your slideshow, then you can display it fullscreen or send the link to someone else.

Each time you save, a new version is created. This way, you can freely share the link and no one can overwrite your work.

You can also download a ZIP archive containing all the required files by clicking the submenu next to Save.

Add pictures

You can add pictures directly from your desktop.

Just click on the Insert image button and browse for the images you want to add, or simply drop them on the window!

The uploaded images stay on the server, just click on the one you want to add it to the presentation.

Synchronize a slideshow across multiple computers!

You can do that in 3 easy steps :

  • Save your slideshow
  • Click the arrow next to Show fullscreen and choose "Within room"
  • Share the link to other computers

The person who choosed "Within room" becomes presenter and when slides are changes, they also do on the other computers.

Smartphone remote control

When doing a synchronized slideshow, the presenter can go to the same URL on a smartphone, but append "/remote" at the end. The smartphone can then be used to change slides

In the future, we'll make a random unique URL to remote control the slideshow to prevent just anyone taking control.
